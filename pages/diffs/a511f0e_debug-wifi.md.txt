 ## Troubleshooting
 
 If you are unable to connect, do the following:
 
-* **Check network connection:** Check that both devices are on the same Wi-Fi
-  network and that the network allows peer-to-peer traffic.
-* **Restart ADB:** On your computer, run `adb kill-server` and then
-  `adb start-server`, then try connecting again.
-* **Toggle debugging:** Turn **Wireless debugging** off and back on on the
-  watch.
