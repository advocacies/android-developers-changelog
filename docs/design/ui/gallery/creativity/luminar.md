![Custom photo editing controls](https://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/samples/creativity/gallery_luminar_controls.webp)

Designed with Android's flexibility in mind, Luminar delivers a seamless editing
experience across phones, tablets, and large screens. The interface moves beyond
standard controls, introducing photo-centric sliders, tactile gestures, and
immersive feedback that keep the image at the center of every interaction.
Whether you're refining details or exploring creative directions, you stay in a
continuous, fluid editing flow.

![Controls and navigation layout adapt](https://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/samples/creativity/gallery_luminar_medium.webp)

Playful, intuitive interactions and film-inspired visual language deepen
engagement, making presets feel tangible and familiar.
Subtle sound design reinforces each action, providing responsive feedback
without disrupting focus.

![Luminar across device sizes](https://developer.android.com/static/images/large-screens/gallery/samples/creativity/gallery_luminar_compact-fold.webp)

Together, these elements create an experience that balances precision with
experimentation, guiding you from the first adjustment to the final image.
