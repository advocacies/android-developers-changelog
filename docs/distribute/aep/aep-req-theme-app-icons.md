# AEP guideline: Themed App Icons Stay organized with collections Save and categorize content based on your preferences.





Implement themed app icons to provide a cohesive and personalized home screen
experience for users, aligning with their Material You system theme.

## Required implementation

To qualify for AEP, enable your launcher icon to dynamically adjust its tint by
including a <monochrome> drawable layer within the adaptive icon XML of
your application.

## Guideline applicability

This guideline applies:

* To apps that provide a comparable Themed App Icon implementation on a
  non-Android platform.
* To all form factors on which the app is available.

## Exemptions

There are no exemptions for this guideline.
